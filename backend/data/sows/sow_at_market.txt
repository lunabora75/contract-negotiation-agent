STATEMENT OF WORK
Professional Services Agreement

Contract Reference: SOW-2026-002
Client: RetailNow Corporation
Vendor: Vertex Solutions Ltd.
Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Total Estimated Value: $1,820,000

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
RetailNow Corporation ("Client") engages Vertex Solutions Ltd. ("Vendor") to deliver an Agile Delivery Enablement programme covering the transformation of the Client's e-commerce platform to a microservices architecture. Vertex Solutions will embed a cross-functional team to design, develop, and validate the platform components.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 Resource Roles and Billing Rates

| Role                    | Level      | FTE  | Hourly Rate | Monthly Est.  |
|------------------------|------------|------|-------------|---------------|
| Project Manager         | Senior     | 1.0  | $145/hr     | $23,200       |
| Solution Architect      | Senior     | 1.0  | $205/hr     | $32,800       |
| Senior Data Engineer    | Senior     | 2.0  | $175/hr     | $56,000       |
| Business Analyst        | Mid        | 1.0  | $125/hr     | $20,000       |
| QA Lead                 | Senior     | 1.0  | $120/hr     | $19,200       |
| Scrum Master            | Senior     | 0.5  | $130/hr     | $10,400       |

Billing is based on 160 hours per FTE per calendar month.
Annual rate escalation of CPI (capped at 3%) applies from month 7 onward.

2.2 Key Deliverables
- Microservices architecture design and API contract specifications
- Decomposed e-commerce modules: product catalogue, cart, checkout, orders
- CI/CD pipeline setup (GitHub Actions + ArgoCD)
- Test automation suite covering 85% code coverage
- Performance baseline and load testing results
- Operational runbooks and handover documentation

3. PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Invoicing Schedule: Vendor shall submit invoices on a monthly basis by the last business day of each month.
3.2 Payment Due Date: All invoices are payable within Net 30 days of receipt.
3.3 Payment Basis: Time-and-materials for months 1 through 6; fixed-fee milestone payments for months 7 and 8 as defined in Schedule A.
3.4 Late Payment: No interest shall accrue on invoices paid within 45 days of the due date. Beyond 45 days, interest accrues at 1% per month.
3.5 Disputed Invoices: Any disputed portion of an invoice shall be flagged within 10 business days; undisputed amounts remain payable.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 This SOW shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for eight (8) calendar months.
4.2 Client may terminate this agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days written notice.
4.3 Vendor may terminate upon sixty (60) days written notice if the Client fails to cure a material breach within fifteen (15) days of notice.
4.4 Upon termination, Client shall pay all fees earned through the termination date.

6.3 The liability cap shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct.
