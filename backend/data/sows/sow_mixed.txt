STATEMENT OF WORK
Professional Services Agreement

Contract Reference: SOW-2026-003
Client: HealthFirst Insurance Group
Vendor: BluePeak Technology Partners Inc.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
Contract Duration: 10 months
Total Estimated Value: $2,640,000

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
HealthFirst Insurance Group ("Client") engages BluePeak Technology Partners Inc. ("Vendor") to deliver a Member Data Analytics Platform, providing 360-degree member views, predictive risk scoring, and claims analytics capabilities across the Client's US health insurance operations. The engagement covers design through to production deployment and stabilisation.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 Resource Roles and Billing Rates

| Role                    | Level      | FTE  | Hourly Rate | Monthly Est.  |
|------------------------|------------|------|-------------|---------------|
| Project Manager         | Senior     | 1.0  | $155/hr     | $24,800       |
| Solution Architect      | Principal  | 1.0  | $240/hr     | $38,400       |
| Senior Data Engineer    | Senior     | 2.0  | $162/hr     | $51,840       |
| Business Analyst        | Senior     | 2.0  | $152/hr     | $48,640       |
| QA Lead                 | Mid        | 1.0  | $112/hr     | $17,920       |
| Dev Lead                | Senior     | 1.0  | $198/hr     | $31,680       |

Billing is based on 160 hours per FTE per calendar month.
Rate escalation of 2.5% applies at the start of month 7.

2.2 Key Deliverables
- Member Data Model and conceptual-to-physical data dictionary
- ETL pipeline for claims, eligibility, and provider data ingestion
- Predictive risk scoring model (readmission, chronic condition onset)
- Self-service analytics portal (Tableau / Power BI)
- Data quality monitoring and alerting framework
- HIPAA compliance audit documentation
- Operational handover and knowledge transfer programme

3. PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Invoicing Schedule: Monthly invoices due by the 3rd business day of the following month.
3.2 Payment Due Date: All invoices payable within Net 30 days of receipt.
3.3 Payment Basis: Time-and-materials for all phases.
3.4 Late Payment: Interest at 1.5% per month on overdue balances.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 This SOW commences on the Effective Date and continues for ten (10) calendar months.
4.2 This agreement does not include a termination-for-convenience clause. Termination is permitted only in the event of a material breach by either party, subject to a 30-day cure period.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All deliverables, code, models, algorithms, data pipelines, and analytics assets developed under this SOW shall be jointly owned by the Client and the Vendor.
5.2 Either party may commercialise jointly owned assets without the consent of the other party and without accounting to the other party.
5.3 Vendor retains the right to use generalised learnings, anonymised model architectures, and methodology frameworks for other client engagements.
5.4 Client-specific data and member PII shall remain exclusively the property of the Client and must be destroyed by the Vendor within 90 days of contract termination.

6. LIABILITY

6.1 Each party's aggregate liability is capped at $500,000 or the total fees paid in the preceding 6 months, whichever is greater.
6.2 The liability cap does not apply to HIPAA breaches, data security incidents, or gross negligence.
6.3 Vendor is not liable for indirect or consequential damages except in cases of data breach or IP misappropriation.

7. SLA AND PERFORMANCE

7.1 Resource availability guaranteed at 90% per month (excluding approved leave).
7.2 Critical severity defects in delivered components to be resolved within 48 hours.
7.3 High severity defects resolved within 5 business days.

8. DATA AND SECURITY

8.1 Vendor shall comply with all applicable HIPAA and HITECH requirements throughout the engagement.
8.2 All Vendor personnel with access to member data must complete Client's security awareness training within 5 business days of engagement commencement.
8.3 Vendor shall notify Client within 24 hours of any actual or suspected data security incident.

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
